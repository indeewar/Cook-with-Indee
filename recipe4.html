Strawberry Margarita

Strawberry margaritas are a refreshing and easy-to-make drink that is perfect for summer. They are made with tequila, strawberry puree, lime juice, and orange liqueur.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces tequila
  • 1/2 cup strawberry puree
  • 1 ounce lime juice
  • 1/2 ounce orange liqueur
  • salt for rim (optional)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Rub the rim of a margarita glass with a lime wedge and dio in salt, if desired.
  2. Combine tequila, strawberry puree, lime juice, and orange liqueur in a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Pour into prepared glasses.
  5. Serve immediately.
