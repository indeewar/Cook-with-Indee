Chocolate Lava Cake

Chocolate lava cake is a warm, gooey dessert made with chocolate cake and a molten chocolate center. It is a rich and decadent dessert that is sure to please chocoholics.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granuled sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degree F(200 degrees C). Grease two ramekins with butter.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar untillight and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the cocoa powder, flour, and salt.
  4. Fold in the chopped chocolate.
  5. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared ramekins.
  6. Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are set but the centers are stlii soft and gooey.
  7. Let cool for a few minutes before serving.
