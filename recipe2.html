Bruschetta

Bruschetta is a classic Italian appetizer made with toasted bread, tomatoes, garlic, basil, and olive oil. It is a refreshing and easy-to-make appetizer that is perfect for parties.

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup basil, chopped
  • salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degresss C).
  2. Brush bread slices with olive oil and bake for 5-10 minutes, or until golden brown.
  3. Rub garlic on toasted bread slices.
  4. Top bread slices with tomatoes, basil, salt and pepper.
  5. Serve immediately.
